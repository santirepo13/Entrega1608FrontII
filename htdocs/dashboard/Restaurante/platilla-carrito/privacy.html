Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are important to us

Last updated: August 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Burger Restaurant ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services, including our website, mobile app, and in-restaurant services.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address
  • Account Information: Username, password, preferences, order history
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, reviews, and support requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, device information
  • Location Data: IP address, approximate location for delivery services
  • Cookies and Tracking: Browser preferences, session data, analytics information

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery
  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Facilitate delivery services
Service Improvement
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve website functionality
  • Develop new features
  • Personalize user experience
Marketing & Communication
  • Send promotional offers (with consent)
  • Newsletter and updates
  • Customer satisfaction surveys
  • Loyalty program benefits
Legal & Security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Maintain security
  • Resolve disputes

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business (payment processors, delivery services, marketing platforms).

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety of our customers and employees.

Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, customer information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Software updates and patches
  • Employee training and policies

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control
  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Download your data
Communication Preferences
  • Opt-out of marketing emails
  • Manage notification settings
  • Control cookie preferences
  • Limit data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@burgerrestaurant.com

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platforms

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
